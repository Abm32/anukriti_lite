# Design Partner Outreach (First 5-10 Users)

## Target roles

- Head of Translational Medicine
- Biomarker Lead
- Clinical Pharmacology / Pharmacometrics Lead
- Genomics Program Lead at CRO

## Outreach message (short form)

Subject: 1-week PGx cohort stratification pilot for your existing VCF data

Body:

We built a deterministic PGx pipeline for two high-impact workflows:
- Clopidogrel (CYP2C19)
- Warfarin (CYP2C9 + VKORC1)

If you can share an existing retrospective cohort (or run it internally with our API),
we return a trial-ready export in one week with explicit confidence states:
`called`, `cannot_call`, `insufficient_data`.

Goal: determine whether PGx would have changed subgroup stratification or risk review.

If useful, are you open to a 30-minute design partner call?

## Pilot structure

1. Dataset handoff and scope confirmation (Day 1)
2. Deterministic run + evidence pack generation (Day 2-4)
3. Results review and workflow fit interview (Day 5-7)

## Success question (binary)

"Would this output change your current stratification or safety review workflow?"

## Qualification checklist

- Has historical VCF cohort data
- Has a planned or active cardio/platelet/anticoagulation program
- Team can evaluate stratification outputs in a 1-2 week window
